Ingredients

  • Maida
  • Keema
  • Tel
  • Banda
  • Pyaj
  • Golveda

Steps

  1. Chop banda and pyaj to small pieces.
  2. Mix the keema with banda and pyaj
  3. Dough the maida the pitho and make it like a chapati but small
  4. Put the keema inside the chapati
  5. Close the chapati mouth
  6. Put oil around the momo dallas
  7. Put water in the lowermost part of bhando
  8. Put momo dallas in above compartments of bhando
  9. Boil the water for around 20 min
  10. Momo is redy to be served
